Frequently Asked Questions

Which bankruptcy court do Houston area suburbs file in?

Most Houston area suburbs file in the same federal district court. Click on your city above for specific courthouse information, driving distances, and local resources.

Are Texas exemptions the same across all Houston suburbs?

Texas exemptions apply statewide, so all Houston area residents have the same property protections. However, the county you live in determines which federal district handles your case. View Texas exemptions.

How do I find a bankruptcy attorney near me in the Houston area?

Each city page above includes local legal aid resources, bar association referral services, and law school clinics. Many attorneys offer free initial consultations. You can also check our legal aid directory.
